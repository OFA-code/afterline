# The Afterline offer

## One sentence

**We turn your missed calls, stale estimates, and finished jobs into booked work and 5-star reviews — automatically, with a human-approved playbook and a weekly report you can actually read.**

## The problem (their words, not ours)

Local home-service owners pay for leads (Google, Angi, yard signs, referrals). Then:

- Calls go to voicemail after hours → **job goes to the next company**
- Estimates sit in email → **"we'll think about it" never closes**
- Happy customers leave without a review → **competitors look better online**

None of this needs more ad spend. It needs **follow-through**. That's Afterline.

## What we install (the "Revenue Rescue Stack")

### 1. Missed-call rescue (60-second rule)

When a call is missed, the caller gets a text within 60 seconds:

- Acknowledges the miss
- Offers 2–3 booking slots or "reply with your address for a quote"
- Logs the thread in one place

**ROI math for owner:** One extra $800 job/month pays for the whole service.

### 2. Estimate follow-up (14-day nurture)

For open estimates with no reply:

- Day 1: friendly check-in
- Day 3: social proof (review snippet)
- Day 7: urgency (seasonal capacity)
- Day 14: "close the loop" message

AI drafts; templates are pre-approved by owner once.

### 3. Review engine (post-job)

After job marked complete:

- SMS asking for Google review (one tap)
- One reminder only
- Negative feedback routed privately to owner, not public

### 4. Owner weekly digest (Sunday night)

One email:

- Leads touched, responses, reviews gained
- Stuck estimates
- Suggested actions (2 bullets max)

**You stay in the background.** Owner sees results, not your face.

## Why they line up to pay

| They get | They avoid |
|----------|------------|
| More booked jobs from existing leads | Hiring a full-time office person ($3k+/mo) |
| Better Google presence | Agency retainers that sell ads, not follow-up |
| Predictable monthly cost | Building it themselves (never happens) |

## Who it's for (ideal client)

- HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, pest control
- $500k–$3M revenue
- Already gets 20+ leads/week
- Uses Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, or even Google Sheets — we integrate later; v1 can be SMS + email

## Who it's not for

- Brand-new businesses with no lead flow
- Owners who won't approve message templates once
- Anyone wanting "10x leads overnight" from ads (different product)

## Your unfair advantage (why AI)

- Follow-up is **repetitive and time-sensitive** — perfect for automation
- You productize once per niche, customize lightly per client
- Margins: high after templates exist (your time → systems)
