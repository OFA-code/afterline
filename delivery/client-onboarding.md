# Client onboarding checklist

**Goal:** Live within 5 business days.

## Before kickoff call (send email)

- Welcome + link to 20-min kickoff Calendly
- Ask them to gather: Google Business access, sample past texts they like, avg job value

## Kickoff call agenda (20 min)

1. Confirm goals (more booked jobs, more reviews)
2. Capture voice: "How would YOU text a missed call?"
3. Business hours + holidays
4. Who gets alerts for angry replies
5. Sign pilot agreement + payment link (Stripe)

## Same day after call

- [ ] Create client folder in `clients/{{name}}/`
- [ ] Create Google Sheet tracker (template columns: date, lead, type, message, reply, outcome)
- [ ] Draft 3 templates → send for approval
- [ ] Set Twilio number or forwarding instructions

## Day 2–3

- [ ] Templates approved
- [ ] Test missed-call flow with owner's cell
- [ ] Test review flow with fake job row
- [ ] Document baseline metrics in sheet tab "Baseline"

## Day 4–5

- [ ] Go live
- [ ] Send "we're live" email with what to expect
- [ ] Calendar reminder: weekly digest + day 30 review call

## Day 30

- [ ] Pull before/after stats
- [ ] Review call: convert to $997/mo or offboard cleanly
