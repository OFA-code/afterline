# Path to $30,000/month (Afterline)

This is math, not manifesting. **$30k/mo is ~20 clients at $1,497 or ~30 at $997.** That's a real business — achievable in 12–18 months if you sell every week. Some people hit it in 6 months with discipline.

## Milestones

| MRR | Clients (mix) | What you do |
|-----|---------------|-------------|
| $3k | 3 pilots → retain | Prove delivery, get 2 case studies |
| $8k | 8 × ~$1k | Outbound daily, referrals from owners |
| $15k | 12–15 | Hire VA for outreach or delivery QC |
| $30k | 20–25 | Productized onboarding, 2 niches max |

## Monthly activity at $30k scale

- **Sales:** 50 outbound touches/week (or 1 setter you pay on commission)
- **Delivery:** 2 hrs/client/week average (systems + AI)
- **Churn:** under 5% — you only sell fits

## What God + skills + AI means operationally

- **Faith:** integrity in offer (see offer/ETHICS.md) — no ripoffs, sleep at night
- **Skills:** sales calls + systems thinking
- **AI:** drafts messages, digests, code — you approve and close

You don't lose because the system is hard. You win when **outbound + delivery** run every week without stopping.

## This month's number (focus)

**$1,500–$3,000** (2–4 pilots). Hit that before fantasizing about $30k. The machine scales; you still feed it.
