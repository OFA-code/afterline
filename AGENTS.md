# Afterline — AI Business Workspace

**Brand:** Afterline — AI revenue operations for local home service (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, etc.)

**Your job:** Close pilots → deliver results → convert to $997+/mo. Stay behind the scenes; the offer sells on ROI.

## Start here

1. Read [README.md](README.md) and [30-DAY-PLAN.md](30-DAY-PLAN.md)
2. Edit [marketing/landing/index.html](marketing/landing/index.html) — replace `YOUR_CALENDLY`, `YOUR_EMAIL`
3. Deploy landing (Vercel: drag folder or `npx vercel marketing/landing`)
4. Send outbound using [marketing/outreach/cold-email.md](marketing/outreach/cold-email.md)
