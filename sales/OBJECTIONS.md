# Objection handling

## "I already have office staff"

> "Perfect — we make them faster, not replace them. Your person handles complex calls; we handle the 11pm missed call and the estimate that went cold. Most owners use us so staff stops playing catch-up."

## "AI will sound robotic"

> "Templates are written in your voice and approved by you once. AI fills in name, time, and job type — same as a good employee with a script. We never send without your rules."

## "I tried marketing agencies"

> "We're not ads. We don't touch your spend. We work leads you already paid for. Different category."

## "Too expensive"

> "Compared to what? One lost $1,200 job pays for two months. What's one missed call worth last week?"

## "Let me think about it"

> "Totally fair. I'll send the one-pager and baseline checklist. Can we book 10 minutes Thursday to see if numbers still make sense?"

## "Can you guarantee X jobs?"

> "We guarantee response time and reporting — not the weather or the economy. What we can show in 30 days is faster replies and more reviews. Jobs follow."
